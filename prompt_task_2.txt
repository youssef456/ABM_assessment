1. Intercept and block all network requests to 'challenges.cloudflare.com' to prevent the Turnstile widget from loading.

3. Inject a valid Turnstile token (simulated or pre-recorded) into the hidden input field named 'cf-turnstile-response'.
4. Simulate a form submission and verify that the page displays "Success! Verified" without the widget ever having loaded.
5. Record a video of the process to demonstrate the interception and injection flow.
